## FINAL SYNTHESIS

This portfolio is a **strategic compression artifact** — not unlike the compression algorithms it showcases. It compresses a career's worth of work into a single scannable document by:

1. **Discarding temporal context** (no dates, no progression)
2. **Averaging collaborative contributions** (single-author presentation)
3. **Quantifying quality** (stars) to avoid qualitative explanation
4. **Categorizing by claimed expertise** rather than objective technical taxonomy
5. **Selecting metrics that reinforce the narrative** (GitHub stars shown strategically)

**What the portfolio optimizes for:** Immediate signal of technical capability and ambitious potential.

**What it sacrifices:** The human story of how this work came to be — the collaborations, the false starts, the time invested, the practical outcomes beyond stars.

**Conservation Law in Action:** As technical precision increases (descriptions of SIMD, cache policies, latency), narrative scope contracts (no room for the story of *why* or *how* or *with whom*). The product we see is **maximally dense technical signal** — which is exactly what it claims to be.
